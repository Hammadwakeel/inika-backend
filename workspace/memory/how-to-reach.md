# How to Reach Inika Resorts Coorg | Directions & Transportation

## Location Address

**Inika Resorts**
Kuttandi, Virajpet,
Coorg, Karnataka 571216, India

## From Bangalore

**Distance:** Approximately 250-270 km depending on route
**Travel Time:** About 5 to 6 hours by road

### Route 1 (Most Popular - via Mysore)
Bangalore → Mysore → Madikeri → Virajpet (~255 km, 5-6 hours)
- Highways are smooth and well-maintained
- Easy navigation throughout
- Popular for first-time visitors

### Route 2 (Scenic via Hassan)
Bangalore → Hassan → Coorg
- Offers serene countryside views
- Coffee plantations along the way
- Passes through small local villages
- Quieter roads with less traffic

## Nearest Airport

**Mangalore International Airport (IXE)**
- Distance: approximately 140-150 km from Coorg
- International and domestic flights available
- Alternative to Bangalore for some travelers

**Bangalore Kempegowda International Airport (BLR)**
- Distance: approximately 250-270 km
- Major international airport with more flight options
- Well-connected with daily flights from major cities

## Nearest Railway Stations

**Mysore Junction (MYS)**
- Distance: approximately 120 km from Coorg
- Well-connected to major cities
- Regular train services from Bangalore

**Mangalore Railway Station (MAQ)**
- Distance: approximately 140 km
- Connected to Mumbai, Bangalore, and other cities

**Kushalnagar Railway Station**
- Nearer option within Coorg district
- Smaller station with limited connections

**Madikeri Railway Station**
- Nearest station to Madikeri town
- Limited train services

## Getting to Inika Resorts from Madikeri/Virajpet

The resort is located in Virajpet, approximately 30 km from Madikeri town. Travel time is about 45 minutes to 1 hour.

### By Car or Taxi (Recommended)
- Most convenient option
- Flexible timing
- Comfortable travel
- Ability to stop for scenic views
- Easy exploration of nearby locations

### By Bus
- Budget-friendly option
- Frequent service between Madikeri and Virajpet
- Good for short trips
- Public and private buses available

### By Bike or Scooter
- Popular among adventure travelers
- Allows enjoyment of scenic plantation roads
- Fresh mountain air
- Photography stops along the way

## Driving Tips

- Start early morning to avoid afternoon traffic
- Stop at scenic viewpoints along the way
- Keep emergency contact numbers handy
- Check weather conditions during monsoon season
- Roads are generally good but can be foggy in monsoon
