FAQs Inikaresorts
Where can I find more information about the resort?
For comprehensive information about Inika Resort, including amenities, room types, and local attractions, please contact our host, Veema, at +91 90357 40031
How do I make a reservation at the resort?
Reservations can be made online through our website reservation portal, by contacting our host, Veema, at +91 90357 40031, or through popular travel portals available online.
Are pets allowed?
Pets are not allowed
Are there dining options available on-site? Can dietary restrictions be accommodated?
Our resort offers diverse dining options and endeavors to accommodate dietary restrictions. Please inform our staff in advance to personalize your dining experience.
What are the check-in and check-out times? Is early check-in or late check-out possible?
Check-in time is anytime after 12:00 PM, and check-out is at 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out may be accommodated based on availability and could incur additional charges.
What is the cancellation policy? Are there any fees for cancellations or modifications?
Our cancellation policy allows for a full refund if canceled up to 72 hours before check-in. Cancellations made up to 48 hours in advance will receive a 50% refund.
*Please note, processing fees are applicable.
Is room service available?
As our cottages and houses are nestled in the wilderness, we do not offer regular in-room dining to ensure the safety of our guests and prevent wildlife from entering the accommodations.
However, in exceptional cases, limited room service may be available. Please check with our hospitality team for specific details.
We invite all our guests to enjoy their meals at The Coriander, our spacious multi-cuisine restaurant, offering a delightful selection of local and international dishes.
