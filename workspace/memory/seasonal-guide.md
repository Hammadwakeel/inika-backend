# Seasonal Guide & Best Time to Visit | Inika Resorts Coorg

## Overview

Coorg enjoys a pleasant climate throughout the year due to its elevation in the Western Ghats. The best time to visit depends on what experiences you're seeking.

---

## Season-by-Season Guide

### Winter (November - February)

**Weather:**
- Temperature: 10°C to 25°C
- Cool and comfortable
- Clear skies and pleasant days
- Cold mornings and evenings
- Light mist in early mornings

**What to Expect:**
- Best visibility for sightseeing
- Comfortable for outdoor activities
- Clear views from viewpoints
- Pleasant for trekking
- Cozy campfire evenings

**Ideal For:**
- Sightseeing and exploring attractions
- Photography (clear skies)
- Plantation walks
- Visiting waterfalls (water flow reduces)
- Families and elderly travelers

**Highlights:**
- Christmas and New Year atmosphere
- Clear sunsets at Raja's Seat
- Comfortable weather for all ages

---

### Summer (March - May)

**Weather:**
- Temperature: 15°C to 30°C
- Warm days, pleasant mornings/evenings
- Generally comfortable for hill station
- Less rainfall

**What to Expect:**
- Coffee blossom season (March-April)
- Fragrant plantations with white flowers
- Pleasant for early morning walks
- Good for water activities
- Slightly higher accommodation rates

**Ideal For:**
- Coffee plantation experiences
- Photography (coffee blossoms)
- Relaxed travel
- Those seeking fewer crowds

**Highlights:**
- Coffee blossom season (March-April)
- Fresh coffee aroma everywhere
- Best time for coffee picking experience

---

### Monsoon (June - September)

**Weather:**
- Temperature: 15°C to 25°C
- Heavy rainfall
- Lush green landscapes
- Misty mountains
- High humidity

**What to Expect:**
- Waterfalls at full flow
- Dramatic landscapes
- Fewer tourists
- Some road access issues
- leeches in forested areas

**Ideal For:**
- Nature lovers and photographers
- White water rafting
- Those who love dramatic scenery
- Offbeat travelers

**Highlights:**
- Waterfalls in full glory
- Lush green coffee plantations
- Rafting in Barapole River
- Peaceful, uncrowded experiences

**Important Notes:**
- Some trekking trails may be closed
- Roads can be slippery
- Pack rain gear and waterproof bags
- Check weather conditions before traveling

## Holiday Seasons & Peak Times

### High Season (Peak)
- **October - November:** Long weekends, Diwali
- **December - January:** Christmas, New Year
- **April:** Long weekend (Easter/Good Friday)

**Characteristics:**
- Higher rates
- More crowded attractions
- Advance booking required
- Festive atmosphere

### Shoulder Season
- **February - March:** Post-New Year, pre-summer
- **May:** Pre-monsoon

**Characteristics:**
- Moderate rates
- Fewer crowds
- Good availability
- Pleasant weather

### Low Season
- **June - September:** Monsoon

**Characteristics:**
- Lower rates
- Very few crowds
- Weather-dependent activities
- Dramatic scenery

## Tips for Each Season

### Winter Tips
- Pack warm layers, especially for evenings
- Book in advance for December
- Start days early for best light
- Don't miss sunrise at viewpoints

### Summer Tips
- Wear sun protection
- Stay hydrated
- Book coffee plantation tours in advance
- Early morning walks recommended

### Monsoon Tips
- Pack waterproof gear
- Check road conditions before outings
- Waterproof your luggage
- Embrace the rain - it's magical!
