## Frequently Asked Questions from Guests

**Q: Is Inika Resorts suitable for children?**
A: Yes. Families consistently report it as safe, spacious, and comfortable for children. There's a play area, swimming pool, and open lawns for kids to enjoy.

**Q: Is it good for couples and honeymoons?**
A: Absolutely. Privacy, calm surroundings, and romantic settings make it ideal for couples. Several cottages are specifically designed for honeymoons.

**Q: How far is it from Madikeri town?**
A: Approximately 30 km, about 45 minutes to 1 hour drive. Close enough for sightseeing but far enough from crowds.

**Q: Can we get food according to dietary restrictions?**
A: Yes. The resort accommodates dietary requirements. Guests mention staff happily arranged simple home-style food when requested.

**Q: Is there Wi-Fi available?**
A: Yes, free Wi-Fi is available. Some guests note internet speed can be slow at times, which actually helps in disconnecting from work.

**Q: Are there activities for adults?**
A: Yes. Plantation walks, swimming, indoor games (carrom, table tennis), outdoor sports (badminton, cricket), fishing, birdwatching, and campfire evenings.
