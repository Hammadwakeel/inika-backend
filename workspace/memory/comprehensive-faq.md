## Dining

**Q: What dining options are available?**
A: We have several dining venues:
- **The Coriander** - Multi-cuisine restaurant (South Indian, Indian, Chinese, Continental)
- **The Cabana** - Poolside cafe with mocktails and snacks
- **Outdoor Cafe** - Open-air cafe for coffee and light bites

**Q: Are dietary restrictions accommodated?**
A: Yes, we endeavour to accommodate dietary restrictions. Please inform our staff in advance so we can personalise your dining experience. Guests have successfully requested vegetarian, vegan, Jain, and other specific diets.

**Q: Is room service available?**
A: As our cottages are nestled in the wilderness, we do not offer regular in-room dining to ensure the safety of our guests and prevent wildlife from entering the accommodations. However, in exceptional cases, limited room service may be available. Please check with our hospitality team for specific details. We invite all guests to enjoy their meals at The Coriander, our spacious multi-cuisine restaurant.

**Q: Are meals included in the package?**
A: This varies by booking package. Some packages include breakfast, while others are room-only. Please check your booking details or contact us for clarification.

## Getting There

**Q: How do I reach from Bangalore?**
A: From Bangalore, it's approximately 250-270 km (5-6 hours by car):
- Route 1 (Popular): Bangalore → Mysore → Madikeri → Virajpet (~255 km)
- Route 2 (Scenic): Bangalore → Hassan → Coorg

**Q: What is the nearest airport?**
A: The nearest major airport is:
- **Mangalore International Airport** - ~140-150 km
- **Bangalore Kempegowda International Airport** - ~250-270 km

**Q: What is the nearest railway station?**
A: The nearest stations are:
- **Mysore Junction** - ~120 km
- **Mangalore Railway Station** - ~140 km
- **Kushalnagar Railway Station** - Closer within Coorg district

**Q: Do you provide airport pick-up?**
A: Yes, we can arrange taxi pick-up from Bangalore, Mysore, or Mangalore. Please contact us in advance to arrange transportation.

**Q: How far is the resort from Madikeri?**
A: We are approximately 30 km from Madikeri town, about 45 minutes to 1 hour drive.

## Best Time to Visit

**Q: What is the best time to visit Coorg?**
A: It depends on your preference:
- **Winter (October-February)** - Best for sightseeing, clear views, pleasant weather
- **Summer (March-April)** - Coffee blossom season, pleasant evenings
- **Monsoon (June-September)** - Lush greenery, waterfalls at peak, fewer crowds

**Q: What should I pack?**
A: We recommend:
- Comfortable walking shoes
- Light layers (mornings and evenings can be cool)
- Rain gear (especially during monsoon)
- Sunscreen and sunglasses
- Camera for scenic shots
- Basic medications
