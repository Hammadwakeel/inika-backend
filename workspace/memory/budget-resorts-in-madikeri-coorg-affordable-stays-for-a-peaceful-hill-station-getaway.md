Why Madikeri Is One of the Best Hill Destinations in South India
Madikeri is not just another hill station; it is the cultural and scenic center of Coorg. Located at an elevation of around 1,150 meters above sea level, the town enjoys pleasant weather throughout the year. The region is surrounded by dense forests, rolling hills, and expansive coffee estates that create a calm and refreshing atmosphere.
Travelers visit Madikeri for many reasons. Nature lovers are drawn to its misty landscapes and waterfalls, photographers appreciate the dramatic views and changing weather, and food enthusiasts enjoy authentic Coorg cuisine known for its rich flavors.

Best Time to Visit Madikeri for Budget Travelers
Madikeri can be visited throughout the year, but each season offers a different experience.
Winter Season (October to February)
Winter is considered one of the best times to visit Madikeri. The weather is cool and comfortable, making it ideal for sightseeing and outdoor activities.
Summer Season (March to May)
During summer, Madikeri remains pleasant compared to many cities. This season is perfect for travelers seeking a refreshing escape from the heat.
Monsoon Season (June to September)
Monsoon transforms the region into a lush green paradise. The waterfalls become more dramatic, and the forests appear vibrant and alive. Travelers who enjoy misty landscapes and rain-soaked scenery often find this season magical.
