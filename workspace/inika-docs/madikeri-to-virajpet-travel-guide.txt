Madikeri to Virajpet Travel Guide – Distance, Location, Route, and Why Travelers Visit This Beautiful Part of Coorg
Coorg, also known as Kodagu, is one of the most beautiful hill regions in Karnataka. The district is famous for its coffee plantations, misty hills, forests, and calm natural surroundings. When travelers visit Coorg, two places often come up in their travel plans:
Madikeri and Virajpet
. These two towns are among the most important locations in the region, and the journey between them is short, scenic, and worth experiencing.
This guide explains
where Madikeri and Virajpet are located, the distance between them, how to travel, when to visit, what you can see along the way, and why many travelers choose to stay at
INIKA Resorts
while exploring this peaceful side of Coorg
.
Where Is Madikeri Located?
Madikeri is the main town and administrative center of
Coorg (Kodagu district)
in the state of Karnataka, India. It is located in the
Western Ghats mountain range
, which is known for its forests, wildlife, and cool climate.
Madikeri sits at an elevation of around
1,150 meters above sea level
, which gives it a pleasant climate throughout the year. The town is surrounded by coffee plantations, valleys, waterfalls, and scenic viewpoints.
Because of its natural beauty and accessibility, Madikeri has become the
central hub for tourism in Coorg
. Many popular attractions are located in or around Madikeri, including:
Raja’s Seat
Abbey Falls
Madikeri Fort
Omkareshwara Temple
Coffee plantation areas
Travelers usually reach Coorg through Madikeri and then explore nearby regions like
Virajpet, Kushalnagar, Somwarpet, and forest areas
.
Where Is Virajpet Located?
Virajpet is another important town in Coorg located
south of Madikeri
. It is about
30 kilometers away from Madikeri
and is known for its calm environment, scenic roads, and coffee plantations.
Compared to Madikeri, Virajpet is
less crowded and quieter
, which makes it attractive for travelers who want a peaceful stay surrounded by nature.
Virajpet is located closer to the
Kerala border
, and the area around the town is covered with coffee estates, forests, and hills. Because of this, many travelers choose Virajpet as a base to enjoy
slow travel and plantation experiences
.
Madikeri to Virajpet Distance
The distance between
Madikeri and Virajpet is approximately 30 kilometers
.
Travel time usually takes
about 45 minutes to 1 hour
, depending on the route and traffic conditions.
The road between these two towns is scenic and comfortable, making it an enjoyable drive for travelers.
The journey passes through:
Coffee plantations
Small villages
Forest patches
Rolling hills
Quiet countryside roads
For many visitors, the drive itself becomes part of the travel experience.

Best Time to Travel Between Madikeri and Virajpet
The route between Madikeri and Virajpet can be visited throughout the year, but the experience changes with the seasons.
Winter (November to February)
Winter is one of the best times to visit.

Cool and pleasant
Clear skies
Comfortable for sightseeing
This season is perfect for scenic drives and exploring plantations.
Summer (March to May)
Summer in Coorg remains cooler compared to cities.
Highlights:
Coffee blossom season
Green plantation landscapes
Good time for nature walks
Monsoon (June to September)
Monsoon transforms the region into a lush green paradise.
Highlights:
Dense greenery
Mist-covered hills
Strong waterfalls
However, travelers should be cautious while driving during heavy rain.
