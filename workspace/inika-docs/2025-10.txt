What amenities are available at INIKA Resorts?
Amenities include Wi-Fi access, a swimming pool, recreation areas, indoor games, outdoor sports, room service in exceptional cases, and 24/7 CCTV security in public areas.

Guests can enjoy plantation walks, coffee picking activities, swimming, boating on the lake, outdoor sports like cricket and badminton, cycling, and indoor games such as carom, foosball, snooker, and board games.

Yes, off-site experiences include guided trekking, birdwatching, scenic tours, nature explorations, visits to Kutiyal River View (3 km away), and treks in the Brahmagiri Forest Range (available on prior booking).
Are evening or adventure experiences available?
Guests can enjoy campfire evenings, estate trekking trails, and jeep drives through the surrounding landscapes for a memorable outdoor experience.
Is INIKA Resorts eco-friendly?
