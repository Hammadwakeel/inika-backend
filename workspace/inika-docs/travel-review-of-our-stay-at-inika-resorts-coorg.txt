Travel Review of Our Stay at INIKA Resorts Coorg
Last December, my family and I finally decided to take a break from our busy routine and spend some quiet time in the hills. We wanted a place that was peaceful, close to nature, and comfortable for all age groups. After searching online and reading reviews, we chose INIKA Resorts in Coorg, near Madikeri.
This article is our honest travel review based on our personal experience—what we enjoyed, what we felt, and why this stay became so special for our family.
About Our Family Trip
Let me first introduce us.
I am Rahul Sharma, and I traveled with my wife Neha, our two children (Aarav, 10, and Riya, 7), and my parents. So, we were six people in total—three generations traveling together.
Our main goal was simple:
Spend quality time together
Enjoy nature
Stay somewhere calm and safe
Avoid crowded tourist areas
INIKA Resorts seemed to match all these needs.
First Impressions – Reaching the Resort
We reached Coorg by car from Bangalore. As we entered the Virajpet side, the surroundings slowly changed. Busy roads turned into quiet plantation roads, and tall trees and coffee estates appeared on both sides.
When we finally reached the resort, the first thing we noticed was the silence. There was no traffic noise, no horns—only birds and wind through the trees.
The reception area was simple and welcoming. The staff greeted us politely and helped with our luggage. Check-in was quick and smooth, which was a relief after a long drive.
Right from the start, we felt relaxed.
Our Stay – Cottages and Comfort
We booked two luxury cottages so that everyone could stay comfortably.
Our Cottage Experience
The cottages were:
Spacious
Clean
Well-lit
Surrounded by greenery
Each cottage had:
Comfortable beds
Clean bathrooms
Balcony facing nature
Tea/coffee setup
Basic amenities
My parents appreciated the calm environment and easy walking paths. The kids loved sitting on the balcony and watching birds and butterflies.
At night, it was very quiet. We slept peacefully without any disturbance, which is rare in city hotels.
Daily Life at INIKA – Slow and Relaxing
One of the best things about INIKA Resorts is the slow lifestyle it offers. There is no rush, no pressure to follow a strict schedule.
Morning Routine
Every morning, we woke up to:
Fresh air
Misty views
Bird sounds
We usually went for short walks inside the plantation. My father enjoyed these walks the most. He said it reminded him of village life from his childhood.
After that, we had breakfast at the dining area. The food was fresh and simple—South Indian dishes, fruits, and hot coffee.
Plantation Walks
One evening, the staff arranged a short coffee plantation walk. They explained:
How coffee is grown
When it is harvested
How beans are processed
It was educational and interesting, especially for our children. They were surprised to learn how much work goes into making a cup of coffee.
Swimming Pool & Games
The resort has a clean swimming pool. Our kids spent almost two hours there every day.
There were also:
Badminton court
Indoor games
Open lawns
These small things made the stay more enjoyable.
Campfire Evenings
One night, there was a small campfire arrangement. We sat together, shared stories, and enjoyed the cool weather.
This became one of our favorite memories from the trip.
Exploring Nearby Places
Although the resort itself was enough to relax, we also explored nearby attractions.
Places We Visited
During our stay, we visited:
Abbey Falls
Raja’s Seat
Madikeri Fort
A nearby viewpoint
All these places were within comfortable driving distance. After sightseeing, it always felt good to return to the peaceful resort.
INIKA’s location made travel easy while keeping us away from crowded tourist zones.
Hospitality – The Heart of the Experience
What truly made our stay special was the staff.
They were:
Polite
Helpful
Respectful
Always ready to assist
When my mother needed hot water late at night, they arranged it immediately. When we asked for local travel tips, they gave honest suggestions instead of pushing packages.
We never felt like “customers.” We felt like guests.
Food Experience
The food at INIKA Resorts deserves special mention.
Meals We Had
We mostly had:
South Indian breakfasts
Rice, dal, vegetables for lunch
Light dinner options
On one night, we requested simple home-style food for my parents, and the staff happily arranged it.
Everything was fresh and hygienic. There were no stomach issues during the trip, which is very important when traveling with family.
Cost & Budget – Was It Worth It?
Many people ask about pricing, so here is our honest view.
Our Approximate Cost
For our family of six:
Stay (2 cottages, 3 nights): ₹9,000–₹12,000 per night (approx, seasonal)
Food: Mostly included / reasonable extra
Travel & sightseeing: Separate
Overall, our total cost was around ₹35,000–₹45,000 for the trip.
Value for Money
Considering:
Location
Comfort
Privacy
Service
Experience
We felt it was good value for money. It was not cheap, but it was worth what we paid.
Guests We Met – Travelers from Different Places
During our stay, we met people from:
Bangalore
Chennai
Hyderabad
Mumbai
Kerala
There were couples, families, and even solo travelers.
Most of them had one thing in common—they were looking for peace, not parties.
This showed us that INIKA attracts the right type of travelers who respect nature and calm surroundings.
Best Things We Personally Loved
After spending four days there, here are our favorite parts:
Peaceful plantation environment
Spacious private cottages
Friendly staff
Clean surroundings
Family-friendly atmosphere
No loud crowds
Safe for children and elders
These small things made a big difference.
A Few Minor Points (For Honesty)
No place is perfect. So, to be fair, here are some small points:
Limited nightlife (not an issue for us)
No big shopping nearby
Internet speed can be slow sometimes
But honestly, these did not affect our experience. In fact, slow internet helped us disconnect.
Who Should Choose INIKA Resorts?
Based on our experience, INIKA Resorts is best for:
Families with kids and elders
Couples looking for privacy
Nature lovers
Slow travelers
People avoiding crowds
It may not suit those looking for party resorts or luxury city-style hotels.
Our Final Thoughts
Our stay at INIKA Resorts was not just a holiday—it was a refreshing break from daily life.
We laughed more.
We talked more.
We slept better.
We felt calmer.
In today’s fast life, finding such places is rare.
For our family, this trip created memories that we will remember for years.
We are already planning to visit again next year.
Would We Recommend INIKA Resorts?
Yes, without hesitation.
If you are planning a Coorg trip with your family and want:
Peace
Comfort
Nature
Honest hospitality
Then
INIKA Resorts
is definitely worth considering.
