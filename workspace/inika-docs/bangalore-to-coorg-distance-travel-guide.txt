Best Routes from Bangalore to Coorg
Route 1: Bangalore → Mysore → Madikeri (Most Popular)
This is the preferred route for first-time visitors. The highways are smooth, well-maintained, and offer easy navigation. Along the way, travelers can stop at Mysore for a quick meal or refreshments. The journey is scenic, passing through small towns and lush greenery, providing a perfect introduction to Coorg’s natural charm.
Route 2: Bangalore → Hassan → Coorg
A quieter alternative for those seeking a calm drive. This route offers serene countryside views, coffee plantations, and small local villages. Ideal for travelers who enjoy less crowded roads and a slower-paced journey.

Best Time to Travel from Bangalore to Coorg
Winter (November – February)
Winter is the most popular time to visit Coorg. Cool temperatures and clear skies make sightseeing enjoyable. It’s ideal for trekking, exploring plantations, and taking scenic walks.
Summer (March – May)
Summer in Coorg is milder than Bangalore, making it a comfortable escape. This period coincides with the coffee blossom season, offering travelers the chance to witness the fragrant plantations in bloom.
Monsoon (June – September)
The monsoon season transforms Coorg into a lush green paradise. Waterfalls flow in full glory, and forests become vibrant. However, roads can be slippery, so travelers should exercise caution.
Seasonal Suitability:
Adventure seekers and photographers may prefer winter for clarity.
Nature lovers enjoy the monsoon for greenery and waterfalls.
Couples and leisure travelers might find summer peaceful with fewer tourists.
Choosing the right season helps maximize your Coorg experience based on your interests.
